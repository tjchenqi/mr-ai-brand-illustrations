# Mr.Ai Character Bible

## Role

Mr.Ai is the recurring IP of the AI 闲僧 video column. He is a friendly middle-aged AI explainer: calm, warm, curious, and practical.

He is not a monk. "闲僧" is the column name and a wordplay connected to "先生"; Mr.Ai is the character name.

## Must keep

- Baseball cap.
- Gray or dark mustache.
- Middle-aged male feeling, friendly and not childish.
- Rounded cartoon face.
- Blue shirt or blue upper body clothing.
- Yellow brand color as the strongest accent.
- Thick dark outline and soft cartoon shading.
- Calm, approachable expression.

## Must avoid

- Do not turn Mr.Ai into a robot face.
- Do not turn Mr.Ai into a monk.
- Do not make him a black silhouette creature.
- Do not make him a formal business presenter in a suit.
- Do not remove the cap, mustache, blue clothing, or yellow brand accent.
- Do not make him too young, baby-like, cute mascot-like, or overly glossy.
- Do not make the illustration look like a generic stock vector character.

## Function in each image

Mr.Ai must perform the conceptual action. He should not stand in the corner as decoration.

Good roles:

- Explaining a model with a small prop.
- Sorting messy AI outputs into clear buckets.
- Holding a flashlight inside a black box.
- Repairing a broken prompt pipeline.
- Weighing evidence before trusting an answer.
- Pulling a workflow thread through tools.
- Marking a false answer with a small warning flag.

Bad roles:

- Standing beside a complete diagram.
- Pointing at a dense flowchart.
- Smiling next to a large block of text.
- Holding a sign that repeats the video title.
