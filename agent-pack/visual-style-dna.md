# Visual Style DNA

## Style formula

Colored cartoon brand IP plus clean explanatory sketch logic.

Mr.Ai stays colorful and brand-consistent. The surrounding explanatory objects stay simple, sparse, and readable.

## Default look

- Clean white or very light background.
- Mr.Ai as a colored cartoon character.
- Blue clothing and yellow brand accent.
- Simple hand-drawn or marker-like conceptual props.
- Plenty of empty space.
- A few short Chinese labels.
- One clear conceptual action per image.

## Color system

- Yellow: brand accent, highlight circles, important concept, warm attention.
- Blue: Mr.Ai clothing, AI/system/tool state, stable explanation areas.
- Dark navy or black: outlines, key props, short labels.
- Red: warnings, hallucination, risk, false confidence.
- Gray: secondary machines, boxes, background props.

Keep yellow and blue recognizable. Do not let the whole image become a single-color blue/yellow poster.

## Text policy

Use only very short in-image Chinese labels by default.

Preferred labels:

- 模型
- 提示词
- 幻觉
- 验证
- 工具链
- 输入
- 输出
- 证据
- 自动化
- 复盘

Avoid:

- Long Chinese sentences.
- Full subtitles.
- Paragraphs.
- Topic titles in the top-left corner.
- Labels that repeat the voiceover word for word.

Longer text should be added later by Remotion, video editing, or design tools.

## Composition density

- 16:9: main subject uses about 45%-65% of canvas.
- 9:16: keep Mr.Ai and the main metaphor vertically centered, with safe space for captions.
- Keep at least 25%-35% quiet space.
- Use 3-5 concept labels, not more than 8.
- Use one dominant object or one small workflow, not a full architecture diagram.
